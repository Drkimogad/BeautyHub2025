Shipping & Returns Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Shipping Information

We offer fast, reliable shipping across South Africa:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days - R100
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days - R150
  • Free Shipping: On orders over R500
  • Processing Time: Orders are processed within 24 hours (excluding weekends)

Delivery Areas

  • Main cities: Johannesburg, Pretoria, Cape Town, Durban, Port Elizabeth
  • Outlying areas: Add 1-2 business days to delivery estimates
  • Remote areas: Contact us for shipping options

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your purchase. If you're not satisfied:

  • 30-Day Return Window: Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Condition: Items must be unopened, unused, and in original packaging
  • Non-Returnable: Opened cosmetics, personal care items, and sale items
  • Return Shipping: Customer pays return shipping unless item is defective
  • Refunds: Processed within 7-10 business days after we receive the return

Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged or defective:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damaged item and packaging
  • We'll arrange a replacement or full refund
  • Free return shipping for defective items
