Shipping & Returns Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Free Shipping on Orders Over R1000

1. Shipping Information

We offer fast, reliable shipping across South Africa:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days - R100
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days - R150
  • Free Shipping: Automatically applied to orders over R1000
  • Processing Time: Orders processed within 24 hours (Mon-Fri)
  • Weekend Orders: Processed the next business day

2. Delivery Areas & Times

  • Main Cities: Johannesburg, Pretoria, Cape Town, Durban, Port Elizabeth (3-5 days)
  • Major Towns: Bloemfontein, East London, Polokwane (3-5 days)
  • Outlying Areas: Add 1-2 business days to delivery estimates
  • Remote Areas: Contact us for custom shipping options and quotes
  • Delivery Hours: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday

3. Returns & Exchanges TO BE REVISED TO THE END

We want you to love your purchase. If you're not satisfied:

  • 30-Day Return Window: Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Condition Requirements: Unopened, unused, and in original packaging with tags
  • Non-Returnable Items: Opened cosmetics, personal care items, sale/clearance items
  • Return Shipping: Customer pays return shipping unless item is defective
  • Refund Processing: 7-10 business days after we receive and inspect the return

4. Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged or defective:

  • Report Immediately: Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide Evidence: Photos of damaged item, packaging, and shipping label
  • Replacement: We'll ship a replacement within 24 hours of approval
  • Full Refund: Available if replacement is not possible or preferred
  • Free Return Shipping: We cover all costs for defective items

5. Exchange Policy

  • Size/Color Exchanges: Available within 14 days of delivery
  • Exchange Process: Return original item, then we ship replacement
  • Price Differences: Pay difference if exchanging for higher-priced item
  • Availability: Exchanges subject to stock availability
  • Shipping Costs: Customer pays shipping for both return and new shipment

6. Contact Shipping Support

  • Email: shipping@beautyhub2025.co.za (24-hour response)
  • Phone: 011 234 5678 (Mon-Fri, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM)
  • WhatsApp: 072 123 4567 (Mon-Sat, 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM)
  • Tracking Inquiries: Include your order number for faster assistance
  • Claims Department: For damaged/defective items only

Important Notes:

  • All shipping times are estimates and not guaranteed
  • Public holidays and extreme weather may affect delivery times
  • International shipping available upon request (additional charges apply)
  • Signature may be required for delivery of high-value orders
