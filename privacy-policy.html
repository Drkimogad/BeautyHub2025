Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

  • Account details: Name, email, password
  • Order information: Purchase history and payment details
  • Communication: Customer support messages and feedback
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Shipping details: Delivery address and contact numbers

2. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

  • Process orders: To fulfill and deliver your purchases
  • Customer service: To respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Improvement: To enhance products and user experience
  • Communication: To send order updates and important notifications
  • Security: To prevent fraud and unauthorized access

3. DATA PROTECTION

  • SSL Encryption: All transactions are securely encrypted
  • Regular Audits: Frequent security reviews and updates
  • Access Controls: Strict permission-based data access
  • Secure Storage: Data stored in protected, encrypted databases
  • Third-Party Protection: Partners must meet our security standards

4. YOUR RIGHTS

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Objection: Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Portability: Request data transfer to another service

5. DATA RETENTION

  • Order information: 5 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Account data: Until you request deletion
  • Marketing preferences: Until you opt-out
  • Support communications: 2 years for service improvement

Contact Us: For privacy concerns or data requests, email privacy@beautyhub2025.co.za

We respond to all requests within 7 business days.
